About Us:

At Enigma Inc, we leverage spreadsheets to efficiently manage a variety of projects. Our utilization of spreadsheets involves meticulous tracking of project details, employee information, job classifications, hourly wages, and the total hours dedicated by each employee to specific projects. This comprehensive approach enables us to gain a holistic understanding of our operations, facilitating well-informed decision-making based on accurate data.
